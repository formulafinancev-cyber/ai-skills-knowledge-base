# SERVICES OVERVIEW

## Purpose
This file defines the main service directions of Formula Finance.

Its purpose is to give all agents a clear picture of what Formula Finance does for clients, so that proposals, content, CRM, and internal workflows stay aligned.

## Service Philosophy
Formula Finance is not a generic “do everything” service.

It focuses on:
- structured financial management;
- management analytics;
- reporting systems;
- operational visibility;
- practical decision support.

Services should remain:
- clear;
- structured;
- practical;
- implementation-oriented.

## Core Service Directions

### 1. Financial Management Analytics
Support for making the financial side of the business understandable and manageable.

Includes:
- structuring financial data;
- management analytics;
- dashboards and KPI views;
- periodic reporting logic;
- visibility into key financial indicators.

This is not only about building reports.  
It is about helping the business understand what the numbers actually mean.

### 2. Management Reporting Systems
Design and support of structured management reporting.

Includes:
- management report structures;
- periodic reporting cycles;
- report content logic (what goes into which report);
- financial and operational KPIs;
- owner / CEO / CFO-oriented views;
- basic automation logic for report generation.

The goal is to make reporting regular, understandable, and practically useful.

### 3. KPI and Metrics Logic
Help for designing KPI systems that actually match the business reality.

Includes:
- defining key metrics;
- connecting KPIs with available data;
- structuring KPI groups (financial, operational, sales, etc.);
- designing how KPIs are presented;
- supporting interpretation.

The goal is not just to produce numbers, but to help them be meaningful.

### 4. Planning and Forecast Support
Support for planning and simple forecasting logic.

Includes:
- basic planning structures;
- financial planning support;
- plan vs fact views;
- forecast logic at a practical level;
- visibility for decision makers.

This is not about building complex abstract forecasting models.  
It is about planning support that helps owners and managers think ahead more clearly.

### 5. Operational Visibility Dashboards
Dashboards and views that help understand how the business is performing.

Includes:
- structured dashboards;
- view design for key roles (owner, management);
- mapping data into visual structures;
- separating noise from important signals;
- supporting everyday decision making.

The purpose is to help see the business in a structured way, not just to draw charts.

### 6. Process and Data Structuring around Finance
Structuring how financial and operational information flows into the analytical layer.

Includes:
- clarifying input data structures;
- basic data hygiene requirements;
- mapping between source data and target analytics;
- helping reduce chaos in spreadsheets and inputs.

The goal is to make it easier and safer to use data for decision-making.

## Supportive / Indirect Services

### Internal Financial Interpretation
Helping owners and managers:
- understand financial results;
- interpret numbers in context;
- see risks;
- see opportunities;
- ask better questions.

### Documentation and Explanation
Supporting:
- explanatory materials;
- structured descriptions;
- internal explanation of metrics, reports, and dashboards.

## Service Boundaries
Formula Finance is not:
- a full accounting firm;
- a legal firm;
- a generic IT integrator.

It works where finance, analytics, reporting, and operational understanding meet.

## Role of This File
This services overview is a shared reference for:
- Proposal Agent;
- CRM Agent;
- Content Strategist;
- Coordinator;
- Colleague;
- Analyst;
- Metrics / KPI Agent.

It should be used to keep all service-related work consistent.

## Final Principle
All agents should treat these services as the core practical offering of Formula Finance and align their outputs with this service map.
