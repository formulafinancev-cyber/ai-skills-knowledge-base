# COMPANY PROFILE

## Company Name
Formula Finance

## Company Type
Formula Finance is a financial consulting and business analytics initiative focused on building structured financial management support, analytical reporting, and operational intelligence systems for businesses.

## Main Focus
The company focuses on helping businesses improve financial visibility, management decision-making, reporting quality, and practical operational understanding through structured financial and analytical work.

## Core Direction
The core direction of Formula Finance is to combine:
- financial consulting;
- analytical support;
- reporting systems;
- business process structuring;
- automation logic;
- management visibility tools.

## Business Orientation
The project is business-oriented and practical.

It is not aimed at abstract consulting theory.  
It is aimed at useful real-world support for business owners and managers who need clearer financial control, better reporting, and better structured business insight.

## Main Value
The company’s value comes from turning chaotic or fragmented business information into clearer financial understanding, structured reporting logic, and more actionable management visibility.

## Typical Problem Space
Formula Finance is relevant where a business has problems such as:
- weak financial visibility;
- inconsistent reporting;
- poor management control;
- fragmented numbers;
- unclear operational performance;
- lack of structured financial interpretation;
- difficulty turning data into decisions.

## Typical Support Areas
Formula Finance may support areas such as:
- management reporting;
- financial interpretation;
- KPI logic;
- planning support;
- operational visibility;
- business analytics;
- structuring internal financial understanding.

## Positioning Direction

The company should sound:
- clear;
- reliable;
- analytical;
- structured;
- business-useful;

## Operating Style
